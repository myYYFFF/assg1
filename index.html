FlexArm EX-5

The FlexArm EX-5 by Vita-Tech is a state-of-the-art, biomechanical arm exoskeleton designed to enhance upper body strength, flexibility, and endurance. Ideal for individuals in physically demanding roles or those recovering from injuries, the EX-5 provides advanced strength augmentation, intuitive control, and seamless movement. This lightweight exoskeleton leverages Vita-Tech’s proprietary NeuroLink Interface technology, translating user intent into precise, powerful motion.
